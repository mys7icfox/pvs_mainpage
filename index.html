My Little Pony

Creative

Join us as we recreate Equestria in the world of Minecraft block-by-block. From the cottages of Ponyville to the towers of Canterlot, you can find it all on our showcase worlds. Become a member now and see for yourself the best recreation of Equestria. Don't feel like building official MLP builds? Thats fine! Join us in our free build worlds as well!

Give back to PVS!

We rely on contributions from our users to keep us running. The server cannot continue to operate without the financial support of our community. Please give generously to keep Ponyvillesquare running! If you give at least $60, you will get a special chat prefix of a heart next to your username on the server. Of course, we're hoping you want to contribute out of the goodness of your heart, these are just fun things we want to give you to show our thanks.

The best way to send us donations is to log in to your Paypal account and send us money using the "Send money to friends and family" option. Log on to your PayPal account and send to ponyvillesquare@gmail.com. In the comments, make sure to include your Minecraft name so we know who to thank, and what colour heart you'd like. We will list your name in public! Leave blank if you want to remain anonymous.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game created and designed by Swedish game designer Markus "Notch" Persson, and later fully developed and published by Mojang. The creative and building aspects of Minecraft enable players to build constructions out of textured cubes in a 3D procedurally generated world.
